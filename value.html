Workforce Toolkit

Overview Value Uses Key Players Metrics Promising Practices Additional Resources

Value

Unique competencies and skill sets are required for providing high quality VR services.  The VR counselors, business specialists, and the administration that supports the operational staff are crucial for the delivery of quality services.  All employees contribute to the success of the VR agency, and workforce management is a part of the support for the quality of the staff and of the culture.

This toolkit section on HR/workforce materials has been developed to provide HR resources for state VR agencies to effectively manage their workforce.  Exploring this toolkit will explain how practices such as workforce planning and engaging employees can make the VR agency more productive, save costs associated with employee turnover, and ensure that the workplace, both in the central office and in the field, is a venue where coming to work is enjoyable and meaningful.  When a VR agency’s workforce is not provided proper attention and focus, it can ultimately have a negative impact on the services to VR clients.
