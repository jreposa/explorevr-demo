Overview

This HR Toolkit has tabs with information on values, key players, uses, metrics, promising practices, and resource and tools to help effectively manage the VR agency’s workforce. 

The audience for these materials includes VR personnel on various levels of the agency, including VR counselors, field staff, administrators, and leadership, as well as others in the VR field who partner with VR agencies.

The largest section of the toolkit is the “Uses” tab, which includes extensive materials on the following topics:

Workforce and Succession Planning and Knowledge Transfer
Strategic planning processes and tools to ensure the right workforce talent is available at the right time.
Recruitment, Selection, and Onboarding
Obtaining the best applicants for positions in your agency and making the hiring process easy to navigate for all qualified applicants.  Once applicants are offered positions, they must be oriented and socialized into your organization as new employees.
Retention
Ensuring you only lose your poor performers and can handle retirements and loss of individuals in critical positions.  This includes employee engagement which helps your employees becomes and stay committed to the work and the organization.
Staff Training and Career Development
Assisting your employees in obtaining the resources and skills necessary to effectively perform their current job and to develop necessary competencies for future positions
Performance Management
Maximizing the productivity of your workforce

Ways to move though (navigate) this Toolkit.

Many materials and topics are included in the HR Toolkit.  There are several ways to explore the content and to locate relevant examples.   The Tabs are the entry into the different major sections of the material.   Links in the pages allow for continuing on a topic in more depth or finding related materials.  A glossary provides definitions for key terms.
