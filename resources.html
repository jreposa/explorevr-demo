Workforce Terminology

 

A variety of terms may have meanings similar or related to “human resources”, “human resource management”, or “workforce.”  These related terms include: “human capital management,” “talent management”, and “people management.”

Human Capital Management-This term likely was initially used to raise the perception of human resources as the equivalent of financial or building (capitol) management.  However, for some, this term tends to demean people by equating them to money or buildings.

Talent Management-This terms views the workforce as talent to be recruited, developed, and rewarded.  Many companies have developed sophisticated talent management software that can be very helpful in tracking many of the areas addressed elsewhere in this toolkit.

People Management-This term was intended to take the word “resources” out of “human resource management,” and appear more empowering to the workforce.
